Privacy policy

BusGasteiz respects your privacy. This policy describes what data the app handles and how it is treated.

Data collected

BusGasteiz does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or usage data. There is no analytics system, remote error logging, or tracking of any kind.

Location

The app requests access to the device's location solely to show the nearest transport stops. This information is used exclusively on the device and is never sent to any external server.

Transport data

BusGasteiz downloads public transport data (timetables, stops and real-time information) directly from official sources of the Vitoria-Gasteiz City Council and Open Data Euskadi. These downloads do not include any user or device information.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any SDK, library, or third-party service that collects data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact the developer through the project's public repository.
